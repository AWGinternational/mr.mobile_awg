# Mobile Services Module - Quick Start Guide

## üöÄ Getting Started

### Accessing Mobile Services
1. Log in to your shop dashboard
2. Click **"Mobile Services"** in the sidebar (purple icon)
3. Choose from:
   - **New Transaction** - Create new service transaction
   - **Transaction History** - View all past transactions
   - **Reports** - Generate commission reports

---

## üì± Creating a New Transaction

### Step 1: Select Service Type
Click on the service card you want to process:
- **EasyPaisa Cash In** (Rs 10/1000)
- **EasyPaisa Cash Out - Receiving** (Rs 20/1000)
- **JazzCash Cash In** (Rs 10/1000)
- **JazzCash Cash Out - Receiving** (Rs 20/1000)
- **Bank Transfer** (Rs 20/1000)
- **Mobile Load** (Rs 26/1000)
- **Bill Payment** (Rs 10/1000)

### Step 2: Fill Transaction Details

#### Required Fields
- **Amount**: The amount customer is paying (in PKR)
- **Load Provider**: Only for Mobile Load (Jazz/Telenor/Zong/Ufone)

#### Optional Fields
- **Customer Name**: Name of the customer
- **Phone Number**: Customer's mobile number
- **Discount**: Discount on commission (not customer amount!)
- **Reference ID**: Transaction reference/receipt number
- **Notes**: Any additional notes

### Step 3: Review Commission
The form automatically calculates and displays:
- **Customer Pays**: Amount entered
- **Commission**: Auto-calculated based on service type
- **Discount**: Your discount amount
- **Net Commission**: Your actual earnings (Commission - Discount)

### Step 4: Submit
Click **"Create Transaction"** to save.

## üîç Viewing Transaction History

### Accessing History
1. Go to **Mobile Services ‚Üí Transaction History**
2. Use filters to find specific transactions:
   - **Search**: Customer name, phone, or reference ID
   - **Service Type**: Filter by specific service
   - **Status**: Completed/Pending/Cancelled/Failed
   - **Date Range**: Start and end dates

### What You'll See
- Transaction date
- Service type (e.g., EasyPaisa Cash In, Mobile Load - Jazz)
- Customer details
- Amount processed
- Commission earned
- Discount given
- Net commission (your earnings)
- Transaction status

### Summary Cards
At the top of the page, you'll see totals for the current page:
- Total amount processed
- Total commission earned
- Total discount given
- Net commission earned

## ‚ùì Common Questions

### Q: Who pays the commission?
**A**: The shop earns commission on top of the transaction. The customer pays the exact amount they requested.

### Q: What does "discount" mean?
**A**: Discount is a reduction you give on YOUR commission, not on the customer's amount. It's used to attract customers or for loyal customers.

### Q: Can I edit a transaction after creating it?
**A**: Currently, transactions cannot be edited. You can create a new transaction if needed.

### Q: What's the difference between Cash In and Cash Out?
**A**: 
- **Cash In**: Customer gives you cash, you send to their mobile wallet (Rs 10/1000)
- **Cash Out (Receiving)**: Customer sends from their wallet to your account (Rs 20/1000)

### Q: Which service earns the most commission?
**A**: Mobile Load has the highest rate at Rs 26 per Rs 1,000 (2.6%)

### Q: Can I process multiple transactions at once?
**A**: Not yet. Currently, you need to create transactions one by one.

### Q: How do I track daily earnings?
**A**: Use the Reports page and set the date range to today's date. The Net Earnings card shows your profit.

### Q: What if I make a mistake?
**A**: Contact your shop owner or system administrator to void/cancel the transaction.
